Expert Case Study

Step into the shoes of a lead carbon auditor. You have been hired by Apex Logistics Group. Analyze their raw data, identify reporting errors, and recommend a decarbonization strategy.

01 The Client Brief

ALG

Apex Logistics Group

Global Supply Chain & Freight

HQ Location
Berlin, Germany (EU grid)
Operations
200 Diesel Trucks, 5 Mega-Warehouses
Current Challenge
Preparing for mandatory CSRD reporting in 2026. Needs a baseline audit.

"We've collected our fuel and electricity bills for the year, but our internal ESG team is arguing over whether we should use location-based or market-based accounting for our German facilities. Also, we bought a bunch of cheap carbon credits from a forestry project last year and want to use them to lower our Scope 1 footprint... is that allowed?"

— Sarah Jenkins, Chief Sustainability Officer, ALG

02 Data Diagnosis Lab

Review the raw data points provided by ALG. As an auditor, you must flag the errors in their methodology. Click the Accept or Reject (Audit Finding) buttons for each claim.

Scope 1 Calculation

"Our fleet burned 10,000 liters of diesel (approx 27 tCO2e). We also bought 30 tons of voluntary carbon credits. Therefore, we are reporting 0 tCO2e for our Scope 1 total."

Scope 2 Geography

"Because our Berlin HQ is in an open grid market, we are planning to publish both a Location-Based total and a Market-Based total for our Scope 2 consumption."

Scope 3 Data Quality

"For our downstream third-party shipping (Scope 3, Category 4), we don't have distance data. We are just multiplying our total $1M shipping spend by a generic EEIO dollar factor."

03 Decarbonization Strategy

After correcting their baseline, ALG wants a 5-year plan to reduce their massive Scope 1 fleet emissions. Apply the Mitigation Hierarchy.

1. Avoid

Recommendation: Restructure logistics routing algorithm. Use AI to eliminate "empty miles" (trucks returning with no cargo) and optimize delivery schedules to reduce total distance traveled by 10%.

2. Reduce

Recommendation: Install aerodynamic fairings on all existing diesel trucks and implement mandatory eco-driving training for all drivers to increase fuel efficiency (MPG) by 5%.

3. Replace

Recommendation: phased phase-out of diesel fleet. Commit to replacing 20% of the fleet with Electric Vehicles (EVs) over the next 5 years for short-haul "last mile" logistics within city limits.

4. Offset (Last Resort)

Recommendation: Only after implementing the above, purchase high-quality removals (like Direct Air Capture) to neutralize the residual emissions from long-haul heavy freight that cannot yet be electrified.

04 Final Audit Submission

As the final step in the ALG audit, you present your strategy to the board. Which of the following statements best summarizes a modern, defensible corporate climate strategy?

AUDIT APPROVED: Excellent recommendation.
