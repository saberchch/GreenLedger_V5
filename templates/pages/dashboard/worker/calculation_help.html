Ignoring Unit Conversions

Entering Gallons into a Liters field or Megajoules into a kWh field. This is the #1 cause of audit failures.

check_circle DO THIS

"Double-check the unit listed on your utility bill matches the GreenLedger input field exactly."

Double Counting

Recording electricity for a building (Scope 2) while also recording the fuel for the building's backup generator (Scope 1) in the same field.

check_circle DO THIS

"Create separate entries for direct fuel combustion and purchased electricity to keep Scope 1 and 2 isolated."
